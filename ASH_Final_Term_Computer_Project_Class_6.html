1.2 QUOTATIONS



Liberty

"The shepherd drives the wolf from the sheep's throat, for which the sheep thinks the shepherd as his liberator, while the wolf denounces him for the same act as the destroyer of liberty. Plainly, the sheep and the wolf are not agreed upon a definition of liberty."


- Abraham Lincoln
(Former US President)



Leadership

"The art of leadership...consists in consolidating the attention of the people against a single adversary and taking care that nothing will split up that attention."


- Adolf Hitler
(Former Chancellor of Germany)
